This is a space to create a handmade dataset to describe a moment with a lover.
You can open this page from anywhere in the world to share in real time.
Your input will be their output and vice versa.
Click around to build a shard of memory.
Enter handmade data by filling in the blanks and hitting enter.

This is currently installed at Axis Gallery in Sacramento.
Should you chose to click "print" while the host computer in the gallery is awake, your moment will physically eject and archive your moment via inkjet printer.
It will be realized in the form of a printed sheet of paper onto the floor of the gallery.
